Why I Love Fitness

Fitness is a huge part of my life. Whether it's lifting weights at the gym, hiking mountains, or playing pickleball, staying active helps me feel energized and focused. Below I've shared some of my favorite workouts and resources.

Fun Fact: Studies show that regular exercise can improve cognitive function, reduce stress, and boost your overall mood. It's not just about looking good — it's about feeling great.

My Favorite Sport

Pickleball is my favorite sport to play. I love the combination of fun, strategy, and teamwork.

My Weekly Workout Routine

Here's a breakdown of my typical training week:

  1. Monday — Upper Body Strength
    • Bench press
    • Pull-ups
    • Shoulder press
    • Bicep curls
  2. Tuesday — Cardio & Core
    • 30-minute run
    • Plank holds
    • Crunches
  3. Wednesday — Lower Body Strength
    • Squats
    • Deadlifts
    • Lunges
    • Calf raises
  4. Thursday — Rest or Light Activity
    • Walking
    • Stretching
    • Cycling
  5. Friday — Full Body / Sport
    • Pickleball
    • Sauna
    • Circuit training
